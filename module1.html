Introduction to Mixed Methods Research

Key characteristics, core concepts, and philosophical foundations

ℹ️  Overview

Welcome to this module! In this module, you will learn about the key concepts of mixed methods research. A mixed methods research design is a procedure for collecting, analyzing, and "mixing" both quantitative and qualitative research and methods in a single study to understand a research problem. To utilize this design effectively, you must understand both quantitative and qualitative research, as well as philosophical approaches.
