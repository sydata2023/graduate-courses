ℹ️  Overview

Welcome to this module! In this learning module, you will learn the fundamentals of statistics by watching videos and online reading. In addition, you will practice how to run a t-test and ANOVA on SPSS.

💡 There is no way to fully understand all statistics within a week. The major purpose of your learning is to get familiar with the concepts and terms of statistics and statistical analysis, so you can study further when conducting your own research.
