A Note on AI in Qualitative Research

We are witnessing rapid innovation in AI, particularly in large language models (LLMs), which offer exciting possibilities for data analysis. However, it is always you who holds full responsibility for data analysis and decisions on data. AI tools can support your work, but your judgment, interpretation, and ethical accountability remain central.
